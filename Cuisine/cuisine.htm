The legendary kebapche

Originating from the rich culinary traditions of Bulgaria, the kebapche is a legendary and beloved dish that has become a staple in Bulgarian cuisine.
Often described as a type of grilled sausage, the kebapche holds a special place in the hearts and palates of locals and visitors alike.


Kebapche is a delicious grilled sausage made from a combination of ground meat, typically a mix of beef and pork, seasoned with aromatic herbs and spices.
The meat is shaped into elongated cylindrical forms and grilled to perfection, resulting in a juicy and flavorful delight.


The kebapche is known for its mouthwatering taste. The combination of high-quality meat, carefully selected spices like cumin and paprika, and expert grilling techniques creates a delectable experience for your taste buds. The outer layer becomes slightly crispy, while the inside remains tender and juicy, providing a delightful texture with every bite.

Shopska salata

The Shopska salad is a traditional Bulgarian dish that is popular throughout the Balkan region. It is named after the Shopi people, an ethnic group in Bulgaria.
This salad is a refreshing and colorful mix of fresh vegetables and feta cheese, typically served as an appetizer or a side dish.

The main ingredients of the Shopska salad include ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, onions, and feta cheese.
The vegetables are diced or chopped into small pieces and combined in a bowl. The salad is often garnished with parsley or dill for added freshness and flavor.

The Shopska salad is not only delicious but also nutritious. It is packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants from the fresh vegetables.
The feta cheese provides protein and calcium. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a light and satisfying dish that is perfect for summer or as a refreshing addition to any meal.

The holy grail

Rakija, the holy grail of Southeastern Europe, is a potent fruit brandy that embodies tradition, heritage, and warm hospitality. Crafted through meticulous distillation, this golden elixir carries the essence of plums, grapes, or other fruits, resulting in a flavorful and fiery spirit. Its high alcohol content gives it a spirited kick, making it a cherished companion for joyful celebrations and heartfelt toasts. With each sip, rakija weaves together centuries of tradition and a taste that lingers on the palate, symbolizing the cultural richness and camaraderie of the Balkan lands.
